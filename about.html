Registration

Register with our registration manager through our official WhatsApp number 08067530098. You will receive a confirmation message and a registration form to fill out. Pay the registration fee of N10,000 naira to the account details provided by the registration manager. This fee covers your audition, training, and contract signing.

Payment confirmation

Send a proof of payment and a passport photograph to the registration manager via WhatsApp. You will receive an audition date and venue.

Attend the audition and perform your best. You will be evaluated by our Directors based on your acting skills, appearance, and personality.

If you pass the audition, you will be contacted by the registration manager and invited to sign a contract with Darknaija Nigeria. You will also receive a welcome package and a schedule of upcoming projects.

If you fail the audition, you will be contacted by the registration manager and asked to try again
